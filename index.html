Transgender Homicide Chloropleth Tracker

Transgender folx are disproportionately subject to higher rates of homicide, suicide, and homelessness. Using GeoPandas project lives to show YTD counts of transgender folx who have lost their lives due to homicide; furthermore, a political component of the project captures the predicted winning political party of each state to subtledly reveal underlying characteristics about these states that can be used for regressional analysis purposes. Project completed for Data and Programming class final in Autumn 2020 Quarter.
